About Us

Pete's pizza started in 1984 by it owner Pete Peteson with a small brick oven and a desire to make great pizza. Since then Pete's Pizza has grown into one of famous pizzeria in town.

Pete's pizza is always made fresh from scratch every morning so, each pizza has that famous fresh crust.

All of are meats and vegetables come to us from locally owned farmers. Making sure that all of are ingredents are fresh.
